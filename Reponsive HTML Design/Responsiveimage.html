The Walk

The Walk from Monterosso to Riomaggiore will tak e you approximately 2 hours, give or take an hour depending on the weather conditions and your physical shape.

What ?

Cinque Terre comprises five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore.

Where?

On the northwest cost of the Italian Riviera, north of the city La Spezia.

Price?

The walk is free. We do not own your legs
